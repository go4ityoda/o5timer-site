# DopeTimer — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 17, 2026_

DopeTimer is a Garmin Connect IQ watch app that provides an on-wrist
countdown timer for precision-rifle (PRS / NRL) stage timing.

## Summary

**DopeTimer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
data.** It runs entirely on your Garmin watch.

## What data the app accesses

The app declares the following Connect IQ permission:

- **`Sensor`** — used to read the watch's built-in accelerometer so the
  app can detect a sharp shake and start the countdown
  ("shake-to-start").

Accelerometer samples are processed in-memory on the watch and are
**never persisted, logged, or transmitted**. They are discarded
immediately after each sample.

## What data the app stores on the watch

The app stores the following user preferences locally on the watch
using Connect IQ's standard `Application.Properties` storage:

- Stage time in seconds (`stageSeconds`)
- Cue points (`cuePoints`)
- Shake sensitivity (`shakeSensitivity`)
- Arm delay (`armDelaySeconds`)
- Audio / haptic toggles (`tones`, `vibrate`)
- Auto-arm flag (`autoArm`)

These values stay on the watch. They are not synced to Garmin
Connect, the cloud, or any third party by this app.

## Network access

DopeTimer does **not** request any communication permissions
(`Communications`) and makes no network requests of any kind.

## Third parties

DopeTimer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising,
crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs.

## Children

DopeTimer is a general-audience utility app and does not knowingly
collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published
at the same URL. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the
latest revision.

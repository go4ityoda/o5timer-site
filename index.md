# DopeTimer

A Garmin Connect IQ watch app for **PRS / NRL precision-rifle** stage timing.

Features color-coded urgency stages, strongest-available haptics, audio cues
on the last 60 / 30 / 10 / 5 / 4 / 3 / 2 / 1 seconds, an on-watch par-time
picker, and shake-to-start.
